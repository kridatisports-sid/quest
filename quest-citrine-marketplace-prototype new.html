Workflow map

The prototype translates the platform document into user-facing journeys and operational checkpoints.

1. Attendee onboarding

  1. Account created with email, Google, or Apple.
  2. OTP verifies identity.
  3. Interests and location tune the feed.
  4. Recommendations, follows, and newsletters begin.

2. Discovery to purchase

  1. Search and filters return live events.
  2. Slot availability validates before checkout.
  3. Payment creates booking and digital ticket.
  4. Calendar, reminders, and brand follows are attached.

3. Organizer publishing

  1. Brand profile enters pending state.
  2. Admin verifies brand and event quality.
  3. Listings publish to search.
  4. Bookings, roster, inventory, and payouts update.

4. Cancellation and refunds

  1. User opens booking details.
  2. Policy evaluates refund eligibility.
  3. Inventory releases and waitlist alerts trigger.
  4. Refund and confirmation notifications are sent.

5. Waitlist management

  1. Sold-out events show waitlist CTA.
  2. Position is assigned.
  3. Available seats notify eligible users.
  4. Payment confirms booking and reorders queue.

6. Loyalty and community

  1. Welcome credits activate after signup.
  2. Bookings earn loyalty credits.
  3. Referral codes generate organic acquisition.
  4. Newsletter recommendations increase repeat discovery.
